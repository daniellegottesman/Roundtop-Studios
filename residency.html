Artist Residency

Purpose-built, not repurposed. Starting from an empty site made it possible to design around artists' needs. Built for long-term stays, it gives artists the time, space, and infrastructure to develop meaningful work.

Residency Floorplan

  • Couples and partners welcome

Private Workspace



Separate from the private living spaces, Roundtop offers 650 ft2 soundproofed workspaces with pitched ceilings reaching 13 ft, operable windows for ventilation, skylights, track lighting, and a dedicated work sink. French doors open directly onto the shared outdoor work area.
